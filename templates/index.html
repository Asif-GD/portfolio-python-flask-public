A short bio

Hello there;

I am Mohamed Asif Iqbal, and the following is a short bio about me.

Firstly, I am a person of varied interests because of my curious nature and gather knowledge across several disciplines. I am a strong believer in the phrase - Knowledge is power. Also, being a person of diverse interests allows me to facilitate meaningful conversations with people from various walks of life.

Communication has always been my strong suit but...

My innate affinity for art made me fall in love with games when I first encountered them at the age of six. I appreciate the artistic and narrative part of a game more than its gameplay. It's this passion for games that made me embark on a journey to understand the processes involved in making games. Later, I decided that I do not want to contribute to the unproductive time sink that comes with the games these days. Games have always been a big part of my life but, for now, they have been shelved. I intended to use games as a medium to narrate stories of my culture to the world. Down the line, I would revisit it as a hobby.

My favorite genre of games is Role-Playing-Games which focuses on character development and narration. Some examples are EA's Mass Effect series, Sony's God of War series.

Topics that interest me are games, although, I try to steer myself away from them as much as I can these days, anything tech, human psychology, philosophy, nuclear physics - mostly physics as a whole, and then the mysteries of the world, in general. Lately, I have been looking up on stuff related to astronomy, world geography, wildlife & nature.

I am an earnest and confident individual who isn’t afraid to think big. I aspire to become a writer on Medium, a YouTuber, a competitive coder, and a software developer specializing in Artificial Intelligence and applying it to Blockchain & Cryptography or Computer Graphics. My life’s goal is to be a polymath.
